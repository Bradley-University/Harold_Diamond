Celeste

A Summary of the Massive Indie 2D Platformer

What is Celeste?

Celeste is a 2D platformer created by Extremely OK Games, Ltd. (or EXOK for short). Celeste is a story about Madeline, a young woman searching for herself who impulsively decides to try to climb Celeste Mountain just to prove she can. The game is known for it's fast-paced gameplay and rigorous difficulty, with a beautiful synthwave score composed by Lena Raine.

Game Levels

Celeste has 9 main levels, each with many collectibles in the form of Strawberries. Shown here are the number of strawberries you can collect in the base game levels. Rumor has it there are more for completing harder challenges!

Level Difficulty

Celeste has 3 distinct difficulties most levels can be played on. They are:

  • A-Sides: The base game complete with the most collectibles and the game story
  • B-Sides: Unlockable levels with heightened difficulty and remixes of certain songs in the games' soundtrack
  • C-Sides: The hardest levels available, three screens of the hardest platforming Celeste has to offer, only available once you beat everything else

Theo

Theo is a supporting character that Madeline relies on during major moments in the story. He's a great sense of comic relief amidst all the difficult gameplay.

Granny

Granny is a supporting character that warns Madeline of the powers of Celeste Mountain. She is often harsh towards her, but ultimately guides her to the right path.

Badeline

Badeline, AKA "Part of You", is a manifestation of Madeline's internal conflict in a physical form, thanks to the powers of Celeste Mountain. She is the games' antagonist, and is often a force of opposition both in gameplay and story settings.
